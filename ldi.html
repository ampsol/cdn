Lightning Data Intelligence

A managed data analytics and data visualization solution giving you data summaries, data reporting, data dashboards, and data insights in real-time, without hiring a data team

  • Easily drill down on your key data from multiple databases in one centralized location
  • Have real-time access to the data reporting you need to answer key business questions
  • Drive business outcomes with data visualizations and insights that anyone can understand

Understand Insights
in minutes hours

How it works:

A combination people and technology solution designed for companies without data IT or business intelligence expertise.

Our business analysts identify and organize your business data 

Our team of data engineers builds a data pipeline and manages your data storage 

Our data consultants analyze and visualize your data to derive insights

You simply access your information in an easy-to-understand format through a secure web-portal and make better decisions

What can be done to reduce employee turnover and drive performance? How can your business allocate internal resources more effectively? What is causing that slowdown in operations and why?

Effective data management can answer these and other pressing questions. Although a variety of excellent data management and visualization methods are on the market, they tend to fall short of the mark when it comes to solving business problems, or are prohibitively expensive for many businesses, with benefits that take far too long to materialize. Many businesses also lack the internal expertise to make the most of their data. 

To bridge this gap, we developed Lightning Data Intelligence – a powerful solution combing business and data consultancy and technology for analyzing businesses data needs, centralizing data, and generating data visualizations and data insights, all at an affordable price, while being quick to deploy.

Lightning Data Intelligence

A Smarter Alternative to Business Intelligence

With Lightning Data Intelligence, you can easily drill down on your key data from multiple databases in one centralized location, have real-time access to the data reporting, data visualizations and insights designed for business leaders, not data scientist, all at an affordable price, allowing you to drive business outcomes within months, not years.

This service is designed especially for companies that struggle with their data, lack specialized data IT and business intelligence teams, and can’t afford to wait for better data insights.

A must for any business that needs data insights for better decision-making today.

Easy to Use

Easy to access, easy to understand, easy to navigate real-time dashboards visualizations designed for each customer to gain insights from their data.

Fast & Cost Effective

Get to the answers you need in months, not years. And save costs with monthly pricing plans and low total cost of ownership. An outsourced, managed solution with no need to hire new data management or business intelligence teams.

Smarter Decisions

Drill-down on your data in real-time to find the root causes of business challenges to make smarter decisions.
